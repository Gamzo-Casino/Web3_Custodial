Questions Answered

Everything a serious token project needs to know before getting started.

We support any EVM-compatible chain — Ethereum, Polygon, BNB Chain, Base, Arbitrum, Avalanche, and more. Your token just needs to be a standard ERC-20 contract. We configure on-chain VRF, smart contracts, and infrastructure for your specific network. Multi-chain deployment is available on Pro and Enterprise plans.
Your Reward Treasury is fully yours. Every unsuccessful round flows directly into it — we never touch those funds. Our revenue share is only taken from the 10% fee on winning rounds, settled monthly. You have real-time visibility through the admin dashboard and can initiate withdrawals from treasury to your project wallet at any time.
No — and this is a key UX advantage. Players deposit once (one on-chain transaction) and all gameplay is instant and gas-free through our custodial ledger. No MetaMask popup on every spin. Withdrawals trigger a single final on-chain transfer. Your community gets a smooth, app-like experience without sacrificing on-chain settlement.
No KYC, no email, no sign-up form. Players authenticate by signing a message with their wallet — Sign-In With Ethereum (SIWE). If they hold your token and have a wallet, they're in. This is critical for Web3 communities: zero friction, no data collection, and a native on-chain identity flow your holders already understand.
Two revenue streams run simultaneously. First: every unsuccessful round — the full entry — goes directly into your Reward Treasury. Second: when a player wins, a 10% fee is taken from their profit and credited to your treasury. We take our revenue share only from this second stream. Unsuccessful round entries are entirely yours, untouched.
